The philosophy

Discipline of the Monkey King.

Sun Wukong was at his most dangerous not when he was wild, but when he was disciplined — the golden band around his head turned raw power into control. Wukong Algo is built on the same idea: a strategy is only worth trusting when it's bound by rules and held accountable.

Every position is taken by code — no overrides, no gut calls, no "this time is different." The algorithm reads the market, sizes the risk, and executes. Then it writes down exactly what it did, and we publish it here. The wins and the losses get the same ink.

Most track records are marketing. This one is a receipt.

How the track record is built
01
The algo executes live

A NinjaTrader strategy trades real CME micro futures. Only live fills are recorded — no simulated or backtested orders ever enter the log.

02
Every fill is logged

The strategy writes each trade — entry, exit, size, P&L, reason — to an append-only file the moment it closes. Nothing can be quietly deleted.

03

Stats and equity curves are computed straight from that log and pushed here automatically. What the algo did is exactly what you see.

FAQ

Questions, answered

What exactly is Wukong Algo?+

It's a live, fully transparent track record for algorithmic futures-trading strategies. The algos trade real money on CME micro futures, log every fill, and publish the results here automatically — so you can judge them on evidence, not promises.

Is this financial advice?+

No. Nothing here is financial advice or a solicitation to trade. It's a record of what one strategy did. Trading futures carries substantial risk of loss and isn't suitable for everyone — past performance is never a guarantee of future results.

How do I know the numbers aren't cherry-picked?+

Because every trade is here — including the losers. The strategy logs fills to an append-only record as they happen, and the site is generated directly from that record. We don't get to choose which trades to show; the algo already chose them all.

What markets and platform does it trade?+

Our strategies run on NinjaTrader 8 and trade CME micro futures — primarily Micro E-mini Nasdaq (MNQ) and Micro E-mini S&P (MES). Micro contracts keep position sizing flexible and risk controlled.
